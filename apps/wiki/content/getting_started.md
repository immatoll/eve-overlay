#  How to start?
## Summary
1. Select start region **MHL-KY-D** for your character.
2. Join the in-game public chat-channel **'SILVER PUBLIC'**.
3. Complete the tutorial (build a **REFLEX**) and jump towards the corporation HQ before progressing deeply.

## REFLEX vs. REIVER
- It's NOT recommended to build a **REIVER **in the starter region for traveling the universe, because it has a far lower jump range and you can get "stranded" easily if you decide to jump around due to heat issues. You still can use it locally for some ratting (to collect FERAL DATA), but keep in mind to leave it behind if you want to progress any further.

### HOW TO START
1. Complete the and build a build **REFLEX**
2. *Optional:* Build a **Network Node** at an L-Point with an **Assembler** and craft a **Cargo Grid II Module** (additional 470m³ cargo space) for the Low-Slot of a REFLEX before you leave the starting area. You can also setup your respawn at a Network Node (right click > set home base) which might be useful.
3. Fill up your cargo with 100 "Common Ore" and D1 FUEL. Common Ore might be needed for temporary storage and refineries (to refuel in a Carbon Field).
4. Jump towards HQ via the in-game map. If you are unable to jump to any nearby system may check for your ship heat.  To lower your heat you might want to get to a outer position of the system (more distance from sun = lower heat = more jump range). Always check your FUEL status and keep an eye open for Carbon Ore Asteroid Belts to refuel. You can use <a href="https://ef-map.com/" target="_blank">ef-map</a> to make a route from your position towards  **Q22|TL6** (~50-60ly).
5. Once you reached HQ you can join the corporation and may also want to place at least a Refuge for a Respawn. 
6. Settle down in a nearby system / L-Point. If you need help to start (e.g. materials or transportation) don't hesitate to ask via Chat.

## WHY TO REACH THE HQ?
- The HQ is located at one of the the nearest station from MHL-KY-D which allows trading (internally/market) and safe permanent storage. 
- Farming in the current cycle can be pretty tedious. At the HQ AREA we can support each other (and new players) with transportation (e.g. currently up to 8000m³ in frigates), FUEL for any ships as well as materials (or even ships).
- Space can be dangerous alone since not every player is friendly in a PVP environment

## USE SHORTCUTS TO TRAVEL
- On your way to a station (e.g. HQ) you might consider using connected systems since they allow you to travel through "Star gates" (no fuel costs). This can save you a lot of fuel and may offers you some shortcuts etc.
- Use the Frontier Atlas or https://eftb.shish.io/calc/path to calculate a path from your location to HQ (e.g. with 50-90ly jump range / still need manual adjustments depending on system heat).
