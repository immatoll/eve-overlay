#  **How to join the tribe?**

1. Join the in-game public chat-channel **'SILVER PUBLIC'**. 

![Alt Text](content/images/1.png "Optional Title")

2. Open the corporation info via MOTD and click on 'apply to join'.

![Alt Text](content/images/2.png "Optional Title")

3. Confirm your application and wait for response from a director or recruiter.
4. An invitation will be received via mail marked in the notifications or can be found in corporation window -> recruitment tab.

![Alt Text](content/images/3.png "Optional Title")

5. To accept the invitation you must be at any nearby NPC station called "Keep".

![Alt Text](content/images/4.png "Optional Title")

6. NPC Stations can be found via Filter "Stations" on the Map. The HQ is also a NPC station.

![Alt Text](content/images/5.png "Optional Title")
